Scalable, stateful extraction engines that convert raw web data into structured business intelligence.

- **Features:** Randomized delays (anti-bot), robust error handling, multi-layered navigation through categories/subcategories, and headless mode support for large-scale collection.
- **Key Metrics:** 1,800+ Products Scraped per run.

### 10. Creative Archive Scraping Suite
- **Logoed Scrapper:** Collects structured project metadata, interleaved content blocks, and video references from design showcases.
- **Visuelle Scraper:** Handles both podcast and design entries, interleaving images and paragraphs for accurate representation.
- **Are.na Editorial Scraper:** Collects titles, author info, reference links, and full article paragraphs into clean JSON.
- **Technical Capability:** 99.9% Data Coverage with Content Block Classification.

### 11. Extraweg Portfolio Scraper
- **Overview:** Extracts detailed metadata, multimedia content, and structural information from dynamic project pages. Handles lazy-loading, media carousels, and JavaScript-rendered content.
- **Outcome:** Structured JSON featuring client credits, art direction data, and high-fidelity video references.
